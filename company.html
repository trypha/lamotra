WHO WE ARE

Established in 2018, LAMOTRA relies on the extensive experience of its Directors ,Chairmen and Board of Advisers that work across several business sectors and are regarded as highly influential in their respective fields. Lamotra is built on ethical values and are driven by the desire to improve communities across Lagos State through establishing safer and more effective ways of working across the Maritime, Oil & Gas and Water transporting industries.

At LAMOTRA, we aim to provide employment across various areas within the sectors in which we operate. Our key employment policy is to drive better opportunities for Lagos indigenes in rural areas where there is high rate of poverty.

LAMOTRA also aim to be the main tax body for petrol stations, ensuring that all supply and service facilities that operate within the Oil & Gas industry and properly registered are taxed accordingly. Our streamline services will enable the Lagos state government to keep track of revenue coming in from these sources.




Quality services

To ensure the on-going provision of quality services in maritime, petro-gas and water transport industries across the state.

Value

Protect and grow the inherent value of maritime, oil and gas and water transport operations across Lagos State.

Youth employment

To generate hundreds of jobs for youth and to proactively create opportunities for them.

Investment

Protect the economic value of these industries and promote local and foreign investment for acquiring better infrastructures, facilities and service.

MEET OUR PRESIDENT

Ajike Aseri is the President of LAMOTRA, (Lagos Inland Water, Marine, Oil & Gas Transporters Association) established in 2018, Lagos State, Nigeria.

The proud mother of four brings over 30 years of experience as an entrepreneur and business owner in the UK and Nigeria. Her footsteps to entrepreneurism began at a very young age where she established A & B Continental Foods, the first London based African and Caribbean Food Store. As her business took a leap of success, Ajike Aseri went on to establish other successful businesses such as ABC Global Assets Ltd, a UK based money transfer company which later delved into business management systems technology. Her other businesses include Great Testimony Ltd, which is a Lagos based beverages company.

The thriving businesswoman also sits as the Chair Director of IMO (International Maritime and Petro-Gas Organisation), a registered UK subsidiary to LAMOTRA.

Ajike Aseri has an Advanced Diploma in Business Administration from West London College, and is a Certified Member in the Association of Business Executives, London. Her vast experience as a certified business owner allows her to bring the very best business management techniques and skills to LAMOTRA as well as her long-life interest in Maritime business.

The lack of effective regulation leaves a great space for mismanagement, lack of health & safety and exorbitant pollution. She found that improvements to these industries would realise employment opportunities in local areas whilst utilizing the economic prospects available to the State.
