IMPORTS AND EXPORT

Lamotra import and export strategy comes under their maritime division. They focus on managing industrial and consumer goods as well as managing intermediate goods and services. All Lamotra import and export services work will include freight, shipping and also by road. The scope of this project is to drive international trade between Lagos state and the rest of the world. Their import and export services will meet international standards and perpetuate economic growth and encourage global business.

MARITIME CONTRACTORS

Under the Lamotra Maritime Contracting division, they deliver hands-on training to their members that operate under the marine sector. They also provide safety Corps Training, which is specifically designed to meet the statutory responsibilities of marine safety. All their safety corps are highly trained and skilled in all areas of maritime safety. The statutory functions of this group are to prevent and minimize accidents in oil fields and to remove any potential obstructions in a timely and efficient manner. Safety corps also regulate the safety standards for partnering organizations that operate within the maritime, Oil & Gas and water transporting industry. In the event of an emergency, Lamotra has dedicated Search & Rescue Marshalls in place to search for and provide aid to people who are in distress or imminent danger within maritime terrains. They provide efficient crewmembers with the appropriate education and training programs that integrate health and safety regulations across their specialized sectors.

OIL & GAS COMPANIES

.

Along with existing Oil & Gas companies, Lamotra aims to develop better floating vessels that will be used by the offshore oil and gas industry to facilitate the production and processing of hydrocarbons and also for the storage of oil. Their FPSO and oil field will allow us to produce and also to refine oil before it is transported into different industrial areas and sectors. Their oil fields will ensure that there is an increased capacity for oil production and then drive the commercial viability of the oil sector.

TANK FARMS AND DEPOTS

The scope of this project includes completion of all documentation, manuals and training aids to be used in conjunction with the implementation of Lamotra industrial facilities for the storage of oil and/or petrochemical products from which these products are normally transported to end users for further storage facilities and terminals. Lamotra depots and tank farms are to run in conformity with the management culture of total quality and ensure sustainable growth and expansion. Lamotra Oil & Gas tanker vessels ensure the safe and efficient transport and delivery of oil to our various facilities. Their Oil & Gas and tanker vessels enhance the safety, quality and reliability of transportation and have also developed a practical training system that allows apprentice crew members to gain hands-on skills aboard in-service tankers.

WATER TRANSPORTERS

Lamotra is charged with the responsibility for coordinating and managing reforms necessary for the long-term growth and development of water transportation in Lagos State, including the granting of ferry licenses and concessions for the operations of ferry routes and terminals to the private sector. These reforms entail the creation of an enabling long-term regulatory environment that attracts significant private sector involvement in the provision of water transport services. Through Lamotra and with the help of other agencies that have agreed to partner with them, like Lagos State Waterways Authority (LASWA), policy reforms and investments to promote and facilitate the provision of water transport aimed at realizing its potential to become an attractive mode of transport.
