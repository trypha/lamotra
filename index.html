OUR STORY

Lagos Inland Water Marine, Oil & Gas Transporters Association (LAMOTRA) aim to be the apex association in Lagos State that regulate the maritime industry, support the affairs of Oil & Gas operational services and manage the water transport sector across the state.

Lamotra run as a Private and Govermental Organization that aims to drastically improve the issue of youth unemployment and provide better work prospects and opportunities for the least-privileged Lagos Indigenes “Lagosians”. At Lamotra, we base our strategies on “The Local Content Policy” established by the Federal Government in 2010 to build the capacity of indigenous firms. Lamotra will work closely together with other organizations to foster value creation in Nigeria’s Oil and Gas industry.

As an independent association established under Lagos State, Lamotra has appointed an experienced team of professional advisers, also referred to as the Board of Advisers. The Board of Advisers have a wealth of knowledge and experience varying across different sectors and thus contributing to Lamotra overall vision and master plan. This Business Plan sets out a strategic framework for Lamotra from 2018 and over the next five years.
